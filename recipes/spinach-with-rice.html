Spinach With Rice

Back Home

Description

Spinach rice with lemon and grains! Easy, fast, economical, healthy and suitable for fasting!

Ingredients

  • 1 kg spinach
  • 1 cup of Carolina rice
  • 1 onion
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1/2 cup of olive oil
  • 1/2 cup of lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup of dill
  • 1/2 cup of parsley
  • Salt
  • Freshly ground pepper

Steps

  1. Sauté the ingredients. In a large saucepan, heat half of the total olive oil.
  2. Sauté the onions, dried onion, and leek for 3-4 minutes until translucent.
  3. Coarsely chop the spinach and add it to the pot.
  4. Continue sautéing for 2-3 minutes over high heat until the volume reduces.
  5. When we see that the spinach has wilted, add the rice and stir until it is well coated.
  6. Add 1 glass of water (the spinach and vegetables will absorb quite a bit of liquid).
  7. Add salt and pepper, reduce heat and simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  8. Add the remaining olive oil at the end of boiling, and sprinkle with the chopped dill.
  9. Squeeze the lemon and mix.
  10. Cover the pot with a towel and let it stand for 5-10 minutes to absorb its liquids.
